Enter your project data and click Generate.

Enter the name of your project folder which you have created in the work directory. #NOTE: It is case sensitive and do not use spaces when naming the project folders. Instead use underscores like this: Sample_Project or This_is_a_project etc.

Enter the title of your project. This will be the heading of your project too, so you can use spaces, letters and numbers here.

Enter the catagory in which your work falls like, graphic design, photography, UI design etc. You can use spaces, letters and numbers here.

Enter the name of your client. You can use spaces, letters and numbers here.

Enter the date of your project like, 1st Jan 2016. You can use your own format of date too and you can use spaces, letters and numbers here.

Enter the description and other details of your work. You can use spaces, letters and numbers here. You can press enter to separate lines or paragraphs in this section that means you do not have to manually add the line breaks for this section in the generated code.

How many images are there in your project? Write in numbers not letters like 1 instead of one.

Specify the format of your images. If your images are .jpg, write (jpg) without brackets and if your images are .png, write (png) without brackets. #NOTE: Do not type a dot with jpg or png here.
